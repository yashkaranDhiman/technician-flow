FAQs

"I'm good at fixing phones, not website stuff. Is this for me?"

            Absolutely! Many of our most successful clients started exactly where you are - great at repairs but not sure about the online world. That's why we handle all the technical website details. You won't need to learn any complicated tech stuff. Just keep doing what you do best - fixing phones - while we work behind the scenes to send more customers your way. It's like having a marketing team in your pocket.

"I already have some customers. Why do I need this?"

            Think about your typical day. Are there hours when you're waiting for customers? Empty appointments you could fill? Every phone repair tech we work with thought they had "enough" customers - until they saw what was possible. Our clients typically double or triple their weekly repairs. Plus, a stronger online presence often brings in higher-paying jobs and customers who are less likely to haggle over prices. More visibility doesn't just mean more repairs - it means better quality customers.

"What if it doesn't work?"

            We get it - investing in your business is a big decision. But here's the thing: every successful phone repair shop in San Francisco started somewhere. Our approach has worked for dozens of techs just like you. We typically see results within weeks - more calls, more bookings, more repairs. And we don't just set it up and disappear. We're with you every step of the way, tweaking and improving to make sure you get the results you need. Your success is our success.
